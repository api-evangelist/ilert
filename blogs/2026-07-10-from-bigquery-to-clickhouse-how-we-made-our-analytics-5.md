Why we replaced BigQuery with ClickHouse: 5× faster dashboards, fixed predictable cost instead of per-query billing, and one fewer cloud provider. For years, ilert has given our customers extensive analytics across their alerts, notifications, and on-call activity, a comprehensive overview of how their teams and services respond to incidents. These capabilities were backed by a separate analytical database running on Google BigQuery.
